Mini Golf Courses

In this game we got to play so many courses as well as have fun competitions with my classmates. I am the type of player that would prefer to play solo in this game. Each 18-hole course is carefully crafted to replicate the feeling of classic mini golf in a larger-than-life location. Interconnected holes encourage you to explore the entire course, search for lost balls and invent your own trick shots using anything and everything in the environment.
